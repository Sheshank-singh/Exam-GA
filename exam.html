JEE Mains and Adv

JEE Overview:
👉 Full Name: Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) 2024
👉 Conducting Body: National Testing Agency (NTA)
👉 Frequency: Conducted twice a year (January and April sessions)
👉 Exam Level: National Level
👉 Languages: English, Hindi, and other regional languages
👉 Application Form Mode: Online
👉 Mode of Exam: Computer Based Test (CBT)
👉Participating Institutes: IITs, NITs, IIITs, and other premier engineering institutes across India

NEET

NEET Overview:
👉 Full Name: National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) 2024
👉Conducting Body: National Testing Agency (NTA)
👉Frequency: Conducted once a year
👉Exam Level: National Level
👉Languages: English, Hindi, and other regional languages
👉Application Form Mode: Online
👉Mode of Exam: Pen and Paper-based Test (PBT)
👉Participating Institutes: Medical and dental colleges across India,
including government, private, and deemed universities

MHT-CET

MHT-CET Overview:
👉 Full Name: Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (MHT CET) 2024
👉 Conducting Body: State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra
👉 Frequency: Conducted once a year
👉 Exam Level: State Level
👉 Languages: English, Marathi, Urdu
👉 Application Form Mode: Online
👉 Mode of Exam: Computer-based Test (CBT)
👉 Participating Institutes: Engineering, pharmacy, and agriculture colleges in the state of Maharashtra

HSC Boards

HSC Examination Overview:
👉 Full Name: Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE) HSC Exam 2024
👉 Conducting Body: Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education
👉 Frequency: Conducted once a year
👉 Exam Level: State Level
👉 Languages: English, Marathi, Hindi, Gujarati, Urdu, Sindhi, Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, Punjabi, Bengali, Kannada
👉 Application Form Mode: Online
👉 Mode of Exam: Pen and Paper-based Test (PBT)
👉 Participating Students: Students in Maharashtra pursuing their Higher Secondary Education
